    How to Play Block Toggle

    • Use the Arrow Keys or W, A, S, D to move your character.
    • Press Spacebar or click the toggle button to switch block states.
    • Walk on solid blocks and avoid falling through deactivated ones.
    • Solve each level by reaching the exit using toggles and timing.
    • Think ahead to toggle at the right moments and avoid traps.
    • Play instantly in your browser — no sign-up or download required.
